UAV Hardware Deployment Architecture

Onboard FPGA + Embedded GPU + Multi-Sensor Integration
Figure 10. Physical UAV deployment configuration. RGB camera, event camera, microphone array, and IMU/GPS feed into onboard processing: FPGA accelerator (SNN inference engine), embedded GPU (ANN forward pass), and communication module (5G/LoRa mesh). Outputs: detection results, tracking with IDs and trajectories, and swarm consensus data exchanged with neighboring UAVs.
