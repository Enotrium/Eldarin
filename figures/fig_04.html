Digital Twin Framework

Virtual-Physical Synchronization in HD Space (Yan et al. 2026)
Figure 4. The digital twin maintains a synchronized hyperdimensional (8192-dim) virtual replica of the physical UAV and tracked objects. Slot-based HD memory uses role-filler binding for structured storage and efficient retrieval. Predictive forward model uses HD permutation: twin(t+1) ≈ ρ(twin(t)). Bayesian updates fuse physical observations with virtual predictions.
